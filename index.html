Our Mission

Bridging capital and reconstruction

The Challenge: Ukraine urgently needs infrastructure renewal, but municipalities often lack sufficient upfront funding. At the same time, international capital is interested in supporting recovery, yet faces barriers that complicate direct investment.

Our Solution: A leasing platform for municipalities, businesses, and donors that allows cities to access the necessary equipment and infrastructure immediately, with phased and comfortable financing transparently linked to EUR. This creates predictability for both municipalities and their partners.

The Innovation: Ukraine’s first leasing model for municipalities, businesses, and donors, created to attract private capital while working alongside donor and public programs. It enables scaling and long-term sustainability, allowing cities to modernize services quickly without large upfront costs.

Impact

Rebuilding Ukraine’s communities with new financial tools

Our platform helps close Ukraine’s infrastructure gap by providing municipalities with access to modern financial solutions for urgent projects. This is not only about short-term reconstruction — it creates a sustainable model that cities can rely on in the future.

By overcoming traditional barriers and ensuring clear, predictable financing terms, the platform unlocks new resources that were previously inaccessible to local communities. This means faster upgrades to public services, greater resilience, and a stronger foundation for Ukraine’s long-term recovery.
