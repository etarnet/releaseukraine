Our Mission

Bridging capital and reconstruction

The Challenge: While international capital is available for Ukraine's reconstruction, structural FX and capital controls — particularly the NBU's restrictions on dividend remittance in EUR — prevent institutional and private investors from deploying capital at scale.

Our Solution: We propose a platform that resolves these restrictions via municipal leasing, enabling predictable, EUR-indexed returns without equity remittance.

The Innovation: This is Ukraine's first private-capital-driven municipal leasing platform, designed to complement donor flows while being structurally independent. It enables scale, predictable returns, and revenue-backed infrastructure development — at a time when municipalities cannot afford upfront CAPEX but must upgrade public services fast.
